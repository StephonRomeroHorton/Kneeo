Learn More About Me

Hey, I’m Kneeo, also known as K1 — welcome! I’m a barber and tattoo artist with about 9 years of experience cutting hair. By choice, I’m not in a shop; I’m a mobile barber, so I can come to you, or you can visit my spot if that’s easier. I’ve been doing tattoos for about a year now. While I’m still working on realism, I can handle most other styles you’re into. Like my barbering, I’m mobile with my tattoo services too, but you’re welcome to stop by if you prefer!
